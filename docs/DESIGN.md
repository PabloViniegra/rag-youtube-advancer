This document describes the design principles and aesthetic choices for our product.

## General Principles

Our design emphasizes clarity, vibrancy, and a modern feel. The dark mode provides a rich visual experience that reduces eye strain and highlights content effectively.

## Color Palette

We utilize a vibrant and distinct color palette.
- **Primary Color:** The striking red (`#FF0000`) is used for primary calls to action and key interactive elements, signifying importance and energy.
- **Secondary Color:** A vibrant purple (`#7C4DFF`) supports less prominent UI elements and secondary actions, offering a sophisticated contrast.
- **Tertiary Color:** A bright cyan (`#00E5FF`) serves as an accent for highlights, badges, and decorative elements, adding a touch of dynamism.
- **Neutral Color:** A deep dark gray (`#0D0D0D`) forms the foundation for backgrounds and surfaces, ensuring readability and focus in the dark theme.

## Typography

Readability and modern aesthetics guide our font choices.
- **Headlines:** `Manrope` is chosen for its contemporary feel and strong presence, making headlines impactful.
- **Body Text:** `Inter` provides excellent legibility for all body copy, ensuring comfortable reading across various screen sizes.
- **Labels:** `Inter` is also used for labels, maintaining consistency and clarity in interactive elements.

## Shape and Form

Our UI elements feature a moderate degree of roundedness, providing a friendly yet professional aesthetic. This subtle curve softens the edges without appearing overly playful.

## Spacing

We maintain a normal level of spacing throughout the UI. This ensures elements are distinct and the layout feels balanced, preventing both clutter and excessive emptiness.
